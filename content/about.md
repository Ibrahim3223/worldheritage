WorldHeritage.guide is a comprehensive platform dedicated to showcasing Europe's rich cultural and natural heritage.

## Our Mission

We believe that cultural heritage belongs to everyone. Our mission is to make information about Europe's historic sites, monuments, and UNESCO World Heritage locations easily accessible to travelers, researchers, and heritage enthusiasts worldwide.

## What We Offer

- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Thousands of heritage sites across multiple European countries
- **UNESCO Focus**: Special emphasis on World Heritage Sites
- **Rich Information**: Detailed descriptions, historical context, and visitor information
- **Easy Navigation**: Browse by country, region, category, or UNESCO status

## Data Sources

Our platform aggregates data from trusted sources including:

- Wikidata
- Wikimedia Commons
- UNESCO World Heritage Centre
- National heritage databases

## Countries

We currently cover:

- **Germany**: 10,000+ sites including 51 UNESCO locations
- **France**: 15,000+ sites including 45 UNESCO locations

Coming soon: Italy, Spain, and more European countries.
